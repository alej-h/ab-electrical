Who we are:

AB Electrical brings over 20 years of hands-on electrical experience serving residential and commercial clients. Our work is built on a foundation of safety, reliability, and precision. From troubleshooting complex electrical issues to installing new panels, lighting systems, circuits, and full electrical upgrades, we approach every job with the same commitment to quality workmanship and customer satisfaction.

We specialize in electrical diagnostics, panel upgrades, breaker replacements, lighting installation, outlet and switch repair, EV charger installation, and complete electrical system improvements. With decades of experience in the field, we understand how to quickly identify problems and deliver long-lasting solutions that meet modern electrical codes and safety standards.

At AB Electrical, clear communication and honest service come first. Every project begins with a thorough evaluation and transparent explanation of the work needed so customers feel confident and informed. Whether it's a small repair or a large installation, our goal is simple: provide dependable electrical service you can trust.
